Galle Sri Lanka

Galle, located along Sri Lanka’s stunning southern coastline, is a city where history, culture, and coastal beauty converge. Best known for the iconic Galle Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site built by the Portuguese and expanded by the Dutch, the city features cobblestone streets, colonial architecture, boutique cafés, and breathtaking ocean views. Beyond the fort walls, Galle is surrounded by palm-lined beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant marine life, making it ideal for snorkeling, surfing, and whale watching. Whether exploring historic landmarks, savoring fresh seafood, or relaxing by the sea, Galle offers a unique blend of adventure, heritage, and tropical charm.
